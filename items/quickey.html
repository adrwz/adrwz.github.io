Quickey

A PRODUCTIVITY APPLICATION.

https://quickey.co





Overview

Quickey is a productivity app that quickly launches new tabs with a push of a key. It was created by a past UCLA student in response to new tab apps like Momentum that do not have much functionality in an attempt to make one's life more productive and less annoying. As a front-end developer, I was tasked with further building the product and facilitating its design as well.

So far, 350,000+ tabs have been launched over thousands of users through our application, saving 17,500+ minutes of people's time. The extension has been rated an average of 4.9/5 stars with 200+ ratings, with people expressing interest in paying for a plus version with highly-requestes features. Because of this, my task was to implement these features and to put it behind a Stripe paywall.

Process
Development

Because the code was not previously documented well, it was a pain to understand how it ultimately worked. Lots of time was dedicated to thouroughly understanding React/Redux worked and how the existing code worked.

Using React and the Chrome Extensions API, I implemented the workspace. I had to balance the functionality with good design and animation. These features are set to launch January of 2019, and hopefully they will prove to be useful for our users.
